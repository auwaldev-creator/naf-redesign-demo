Recruitment Requirements

Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding.

Important Notice

Application Process is FREE OF CHARGE. No payment should be made at any point. Any applicant suspected of impersonation or submitting false documents shall be disqualified and prosecuted.

General Requirements

Nationality

Applicants must be Nigerian citizens by birth.

Age

18 - 22 years (Non-tradesmen), 18 - 25 years (Tradesmen).

Height

Male: 1.66m, Female: 1.63m.

Medical Fitness

Must be medically, physically, and psychologically fit.

Educational Requirements

Non-Tradesmen/Women

Minimum of 5 credits including Mathematics and English in not more than 2 sittings in WAEC/NECO/GCE/NABTEB.

Tradesmen/Women

ND/NCE with at least lower credit, or Trade Test Certificate, plus 5 credits including Maths & English.

Acknowledgement

Before you can apply, you must acknowledge that you have read and understood all guidelines.

  • I meet all eligibility criteria.
  • All information I will provide is true.
  • I understand that this application is free.
You must accept the guidelines.
