NAF Recruitment Requirements 2025

Please read all instructions carefully before proceeding with your application

Online Registration: 14 October 2025 - 25 November 2025

Important Notice

Application Process is FREE OF CHARGE and no payment should be made at any point throughout the Exercise.

Any applicant suspected to have impersonated or submitted false document(s) shall be disqualified and handed over to the Nigeria Police for prosecution.

General Requirements

Nationality

Applicants must be Nigerian citizens

Age Requirements

Non-tradesmen/women: 18 - 22 years

Tradesmen/women: 18 - 27 years

Marital Status

Must be single and have no children

Height Requirements

Males: Not less than 1.66m

Females: Not less than 1.63m

Medical Fitness

Must be medically, physically and psychologically fit

Local Government Attestation

Form must be signed by authorized personnel

Educational Requirements

Non-Tradesmen/Women

Minimum of 5 credits including Mathematics and English Language in not more than 2 sittings in:

  • WAEC/NECO
  • GCE/NABTEB
  • NBAIS

Tradesmen/Women

ND/NCE with at least lower credit + 5 credits including Mathematics and English

OR

Trade Test Certificate + 5 credits including Mathematics and English

Required Documents

Identification

  • Birth Certificate/Declaration of Age
  • Indigeneship Certificate
  • 2 Recent Passport Photographs

Educational

  • Original & photocopy of certificates
  • Secondary School Testimonial
  • Professional Certificates (if any)

Other Documents

  • Local Government Attestation Form
  • Parent/Guardian Consent Form
  • Medical Certificate

Declaration

Guidelines Acknowledgement

  • I meet all the eligibility criteria specified

  • I understand that false declaration may lead to disqualification and prosecution
  • I acknowledge that the application process is FREE OF CHARGE
